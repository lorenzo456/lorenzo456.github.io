About me

Hey, welcome to my portfolio website! I'm Lorenzo James, a game developer from the netherlands. I'm foremost a game programmer, but I also enjoy different aspects of game development such as game design and Quality assurance.

My background in Programming started when I started a Media technology course at my local University, University of applied sciences Rotterdam. In the year I was studying there I've learned the basics of programming and more specifically web development. During my year attending this course, I found my passion for game developing growing more than my passion for web development, so I decided to pursue it.

Now I'm a 3rd year Game design and development student at the University of the arts Utrecht and my interest for game development is quite broad, I'm interested in developing all types of games, for any kind of genres, from pc action games to applied games on mobile devices. I enjoy developing the kind of games which will either create unique experiences or games which the end user will truly enjoy.

Besides Game development I enjoy drawing, movies, traveling, football, challenging myself and learning new things. I am always open for journeys, challenges and new experiences, making me flexible. I am now also actively looking for internship opportunities.
